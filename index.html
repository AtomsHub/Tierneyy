About Us

Tierney Air is a family-owned and operated air conditioning business proudly based in Brisbane. We specialise in the installation, maintenance, and repair of both split system and ducted air conditioning units. With a strong commitment to quality workmanship and reliable service, we’re dedicated to keeping homes, small businesses, and rental properties across Brisbane comfortable all year round. Our goal is simple: deliver expert solutions with the friendly, personalised care you can only get from a family business.

Tierney Air – Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What brands do you install?
A: We specialize in Daikin, Mitsubishi, Panasonic, and other top-tier brands – all with full manufacturer warranties.

Q: How long does a new AC installation take?
A: Split systems: 4–6 hours. Ducted systems: 1–2 days. Minimal disruption guaranteed.

Q: Do you handle council permits for ducted systems?
A: Yes – we manage all Brisbane City Council paperwork for you.
Q: What’s your emergency response time?
A: Most Brisbane metro areas: 2–4 hours. 24/7 availability.

Q: Do you charge call-out fees?
A: Never. We diagnose first, quote upfront – you approve before we proceed.

Q: Can you fix [brand] units?
A: Yes – we repair all makes and models, including older systems.
Q: How often should I service my AC?
A: Annually for homes. Quarterly for commercial systems.

Q: What’s included in a maintenance check?
A: Full system inspection, filter cleaning, refrigerant check, and performance optimization.

Q: Do you offer maintenance plans?
A: Yes – our Priority Care Club gives you 15% off repairs and priority booking.
Q: What’s the average cost for a new split system?
A: $1,500–$4,500 installed, depending on size and brand. Free exact quotes.

Q: Do you price-match competitors?
A: Yes – show us a written quote and we’ll beat it by 5%.
Q: Are your installations guaranteed?
A: Yes – 5-year workmanship warranty on all installations.

Q: What if a repaired part fails?
A: All replacement parts come with 12-month warranties.
Q: Do you service [suburb]?
A: We cover all Brisbane suburbs from Redcliffe to Logan. Call to confirm.

Q: Do you work weekends?
A: Yes – standard hours 7am–7pm, 24/7 for emergencies.
Q: Are your technicians licensed?
A: All QBCC licensed and ARCtick certified – ask to see credentials.

Q: Why choose Tierney over big franchises?
A: Local owners on every job – no subcontractors, no upsells.
